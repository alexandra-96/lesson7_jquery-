Veganize Betty Crocker Cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegan butter
  • Vegan egg replacer
  • 1 package Betty Crocker cookie mix
  • 1 c broken walnuts (optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325.

Soften vegan butter

Create vegan egg in seperate bowl(according to box instructions)

Stir in vegan egg

Fold in softened vegan butter

Add walnuts (if using), then stir once or twice.

Flour a cookie pan and scoop out quarter sized balls of dough

Bake on center rack for 12 minutes
